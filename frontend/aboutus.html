About LocalBiz Connect

A simple full-stack learning project for beginners

Project Idea

LocalBiz Connect helps a small business display services online and collect enquires from customer

Why we are building this

This project helps students understand how a real web project grows:HTML structure,CSS design,JavaScript interaction,backenedAPI,database and final submission.

Future Scope

In later days,the contact form can be connected tot Flask backend and SQLite database.Then the dashboard can show real enquiry data
