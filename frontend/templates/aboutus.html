About LocalBiz Connect

LocalBiz Connect is a fast, lightweight website built to help neighborhood businesses grow. It gives local service providers a clean digital storefront to showcase their work and easily collect customer messages. No complex setups, no unnecessary bloat—just a simple, powerful tool to connect local experts with local clients.

Project Idea

LocalBiz Connect helps a small business display services online and collect enquiries from customers.

Why We Are Building This

This project helps students understand how a real web project grows: HTML structure, CSS design, JavaScript interaction, backend API, database, and final submission.

Future Scope

In later stages, the contact form can be connected to a Flask backend and SQLite database. Then the dashboard can show real enquiry data.
